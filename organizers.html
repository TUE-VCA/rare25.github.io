👨‍💻 Organizing Team 👨‍💻

RARE25 was initiated within the BONSAI consortium—an interdisciplinary collaboration spanning over a decade and uniting numerous academic hospitals and technical universities. Led by Amsterdam Medical Center and Eindhoven University of Technology, BONSAI focuses on advancing clinical decision-making through biomedical image analysis and AI. RARE25 specifically targets the challenge of detecting low-prevalence cancers in Barrett’s esophagus during endoscopic procedures—a critical yet under-addressed need in early cancer detection. The challenge builds on BONSAI’s extensive experience in developing and validating AI-assisted tools in real-world clinical settings, and brings together leading experts in gastroenterology and machine learning.
